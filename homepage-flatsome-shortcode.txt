<p>We maintain clear records and make our financial statements and Constitution available to our members.</p>

[col span="7" span__sm="12"]
[accordion]
[accordion-item title="How are decisions made?"]
[ux_text]
<p>The committee makes day-to-day decisions. Significant changes to our rules require a special resolution (75% agreement) by members at a general meeting with 14 days' notice.</p>
[/ux_text]
[/accordion-item]
[accordion-item title="How are funds managed?"]
[ux_text]
<p>We can raise money through fees, donations, grants, and cultural partnerships. The Treasurer oversees day-to-day finances, and if annual revenue exceeds $500,000, we undertake independent audits.</p>
[/ux_text]
[/accordion-item]
[accordion-item title="What is the Code of Conduct?"]
[ux_text]
<p>Everyone is expected to act with honesty, integrity, and respect for the cultures that make our community who we are. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed.</p>
