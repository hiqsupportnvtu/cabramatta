Eligibility Criteria

As per Clause 8.4 of our Constitution, membership is open to individuals and entities that align with our purposes.

1

Full Membership

Must be an incorporated body, a registered company, or a sole trader actively conducting business within Cabramatta. Full members have the right to vote and hold executive office.

2

Associate Membership

Open to individuals, residents, and members of the general public who support the purposes of the Association. Associate members cannot vote but can attend general meetings.

3

Affiliate Membership

Open to other organisations, educational institutions, or government bodies who wish to partner with us. Cannot vote, but formalises a strategic relationship.
