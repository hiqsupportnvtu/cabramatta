About the Association

Discover our history, our purpose, and the dedicated team working to elevate Cabramatta's multicultural business landscape.

Our Story

The Cabramatta Business Association is an incorporated Not-for-Profit body created to represent the vibrant and diverse business community of Cabramatta. Founded on the principles of unity and cultural preservation, we strive to make Cabramatta an internationally recognised destination.

Our motto, "Stronger Together", reflects our belief that by weaving traditional cultural heritage with modern enterprise, we can overcome challenges and create lasting economic resilience.

Our Core Objectives

  • Advocate for small multicultural businesses
  • Secure Special Entertainment Precinct status
  • Promote Cabramatta as a tourism & food destination
  • Deliver business development and training

Cultural Advisory Council (CAC)

"Ensuring our decisions are deeply rooted in the community's heritage."

As per our Constitution, the CAC is an esteemed body comprising community elders, arts practitioners, youth representatives, and business leaders. Their role is to provide non-binding, strategic advice to the Management Committee regarding cultural affairs, diversity, and community harmony.
